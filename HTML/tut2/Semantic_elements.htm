Semantic elements

Semantic elements means element with a meaning.

Sematic elements defines its meaning to both browser and the developer.

The example of non-sematic elements are div and span which doesn't defined its meaning to the developer.

The example of semantic elements are form,table, and article etc.we can tell the meaning of the tag just by their names.
